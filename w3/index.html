How to Pick Your First Art Program

Choosing an art program is super overwhelming because there are so many options like Photoshop, Krita, and Procreate. I personally started with something free because I didn't want to spend money before I knew what I was doing.

The most important thing is to just start drawing. Don't worry too much about the fancy brushes yet. Just find a pen tool you like and start making lines!
